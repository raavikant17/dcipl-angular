Overview

Darwish Cybertech India Private Limited (DCIPL) is 100% subsidiary of United Motors Heavy Equipment LLC (UMHE), Dubai, United Arab Emirates (UAE). UMHE is a group company belonging to large enterprise Darwish Bin Ahmed & Sons Group (DBA & Sons), UAE. DCIPL at present is focused on providing the captive Information Technology (IT) and IT Enabled Services (ITES) solutions for the group, enabling them to leverage IT as a strategic asset. Empowered by a unique combination of skilled professionals, teamwork, strong leadership, and standards of service, through the depth in expertise of information technology verticals, sound & scalable IT infrastructure, Business Intelligence and planning tools, we have created a conducive environment to fulfill demand of cost control and efficiency, as well as the business’ need for unwavering focus to provide agile, approachable, and adaptable IT & IT enabled services.
